How to make chocolate chip cookies.

Cookies are a type of biscuit, which are extremely addictive, for there amazing taste. Oh without a doubt, you will be putting your hand in the cookie jar, one after the other. Although this is very bad for you as they contain a numerous amount of calories. But given in the right quantity with a balanced diet, these can be a very enjoyable experience especially if they are homemade!

Ingredients

  • 100g (4oz) of butter or margierience.
  • 100g (4oz) caster surgar.
  • 1 egg.
  • half a teaspoon of vanilla essance.
  • 175 (6oz) plain flour.
  • 175g (6oz) choclate chips.

preparation

  1. Grease two large baking sheets with butter or margerirence. Turn your over onto 170c/350f/Gas mark 4.
  2. Put the sugar and butter or margerience into a large bowl. Beat it briskly until it is light and creamy. (use a wooden spoon to do this)
  3. break the egg into a small bowl and beat it well. Stir in the vanilla essance, then add the mixture to the large bowl.
  4. Sift the flour into a large bowl and stir well to make a smooth mixture. Stir in a 100g (4oz) of the choclate chips.
  5. Put a heaped table spoon of the mixture onto a baking sheet. Use up the rest of the mixture to make 11 more cookies. press down on each cookie with a spoon.
  6. flatten each cookie slightly with the back of a fork. Sprinkle the top of each one with some of the remaining choclate chips.
  7. bake the cookies for 10-15 minutes. They should be pale goldenbrown and slightly soft in the middle.
  8. leave the cookies for a few minutes and then use a spatula or fish slice to lift them onto a wire wrack Leave them to cool.
